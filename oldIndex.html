DIGITAL SUNSET STUDIOS

Purely a passion project, Digital Sunset Studios is the name I work under for my various software projects. Here you'll find infomation and links to my Virtual Reality projects, hardware projects , and Web Development projects.


In 2013 I first started playing with electronics, then later after winning a Google programming scholarship I started to combine this with software programming.

After taking an intensive 9 week Web Dev bootcamp in 2019 I have been working as a full time web developer, but at night I put on my glasses and become a VR Developer as well as creating hardware synthersizers for fun.

I currently live in Leipzig, Germany. Learn more here.
